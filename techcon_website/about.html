Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and innovators. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated global tech conferences, bringing together leaders from startups to Fortune 500 companies.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower technology-driven change through community, education, and innovation. We strive to provide a platform for thinkers and doers to collaborate, learn, and inspire one another.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Dr. Lovelace spoke on the evolution of algorithms and their ethical implications in modern AI systems.

Linus Torvalds

Creator of Linux, Linus inspired the open-source movement and shared lessons from decades of building developer communities.

Ginni Rometty

Ginni discussed corporate responsibility in the era of AI and the importance of inclusive leadership in tech.
